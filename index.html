Covid-19

Prevention Actions

Some of the most simple things you can do to decrease your chances of contracting the virus is practicing hand hygiene, wearing a high-quality mask, and keeping your distance, when possible, from the person who is sick or who tested positive. Improving ventilation and filtration can help prevent virus particles from accumulating in indoor air. Improving ventilation and filtration can help protect you from getting infected with and spreading the virus that causes COVID-19. Getting tested for Covid-19 if you are showing symptoms of the virus can help you prevent spreading the virus to others.

The Many Effects of the Pandemic

The pandemic has led to an increase in job loss and financial instability with the hospitality industry suffering the greatest job loss. This event of increased job loss resulted from the closing of stores or business lost. Another very serious issue as a result of the pandemic has been the stock shortage of many daily essentials such as cleaning products, masks, tissues and paper towls.
